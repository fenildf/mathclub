ABOUT

Like playing pretend? Or arguing? Or arguing about the correct way to play pretend? In either case, math (or math club) can offer you that and

  • Profound aesthetic beauty (see officers section)
  • Profound aesthetic mathematical beauty
  • Lectures from brilliant mathematicians

We hold weekly meetings Tuesdays at 7:30 in the Barn (the big room on the third floor of Ryerson) featuring the above and talks from student members as well.
